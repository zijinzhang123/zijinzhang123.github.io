I am an Assistant Professor of Business Analytics at the Carroll School of Management, Boston College. I received my Ph.D. in Technology & Operations from the Stephen M. Ross School of Business at the University of Michigan.

My research interests are in revenue management and public policy, with a focus on improving data-driven decision-making in business (inventory management and pricing analytics) and societal operations (education and learning, government resource allocation, and AI regulation). Methodologically, I develop stochastic models and machine learning algorithms that use optimization and statistics tools to address operational problems under uncertainty.

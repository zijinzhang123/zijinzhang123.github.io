About me

I'm currently a fifth-year Ph.D. student at the University of Michigan, fortunate to be advised by Professor Hyun-Soo Ahn and Professor Lennart Baardman. My research focuses on operational efficiency and strategic decision-making across various sectors, including retail management, supply chain management, and non-profit operations. In my recent research, I am particularly interested in understanding: (i) how data can enhance algorithmic decision-making and provide valuable insights for policy design, and (ii) identifying the most effective data for informed decision-making. Through my work, I aim to develop innovative solutions and theoretical insights that address complex challenges and improve overall service performance.
